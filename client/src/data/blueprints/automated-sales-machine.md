# The Automated Sales Machine: Signal-Based Outreach with AI in 2026

How to combine AI Scraping, ElevenLabs v3, and Amplemarket to build a high-conversion outbound engine.

## Step 1: Data Sourcing

Use AI scraping tools to build your lead list from intent signals. Our top picks:

- **Browse AI** — No-code extraction from directories, job boards, and competitor sites. Schedule daily runs and export to CSV.
- **Apollo** — 275M+ contacts with AI-assisted search and waterfall enrichment for verified emails.
- **Clay** — Waterfall enrichment and hyper-targeted lists when you need 1:1 personalization at scale.

## Step 2: Personalized Content

Generate voiceovers and narration with ElevenLabs v3. Use audio tags for character direction and emotional nuance. See our [Eleven v3 Audio Tags & Character Direction Guide](/blog/mastering-eleven-v3-audio-tags-character-direction-2026) for the full cheat sheet.

## Step 3: Content Creation

Create matching ad creatives with [AdCreative.ai](/go/adcreative). Generate on-brand images and copy in minutes for rapid A/B testing across Meta and Google.

**Post-Production:** Use [Descript](/go/descript) to sync your ElevenLabs audio with b-roll. Use the 'Studio Sound' feature to ensure your AI-generated voice sounds like it was recorded in a $10k booth.

## Step 4: Execution

Run your sequences with [Amplemarket](/go/amplemarket). AI-powered sales intelligence and outreach to deliver personalized campaigns at scale.

## The 2026 Video Agent Workflow

1. Use [MindStudio](/go/mindstudio) to generate a personalized video script based on [Apollo.io](/go/apollo) lead data.
2. Import the script into [Descript](/go/descript). Use the 'Underdub' feature to voice the script using your [ElevenLabs](/go/elevenlabs) clone.

**Goal:** Create 100 personalized sales videos in 10 minutes.
